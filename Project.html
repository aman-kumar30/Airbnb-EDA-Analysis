Insights from Visualization¶

States with Highest Average Prices:¶

Punjab, Andaman & Nicobar Islands, and Bihar appear to have the highest average Airbnb prices. This might indicate premium properties or low availability leading to high average prices.¶

Mid-Range States:¶

Delhi, Rajasthan, Himachal Pradesh, and Andhra Pradesh fall in the mid-price range. These states might have a mix of budget and premium properties.¶

States with Lower Average Prices:¶

Karnataka, Goa, Maharashtra, Kerala, Gujarat, and Tamil Nadu tend to have lower average prices despite being popular tourist destinations. This could be due to a larger number of listings, creating competitive pricing.¶

Possible Reasons for Variations:¶

-States with very few listings (like Andaman & Nicobar) may show inflated averages.¶
-Tourist-heavy states like Goa and Kerala likely have more budget-friendly options, reducing the mean price.¶

Business Implications:¶

-Investors can target Punjab and Bihar for high-end properties.¶
-States like Goa and Kerala may be better for volume-based hosting, as there are more budget travelers.¶
